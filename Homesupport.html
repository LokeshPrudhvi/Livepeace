Household chores that the senior people used to do effortlessly become tiring and difficult at some point. This may be due to fatigue or illness. This care package therefore ensures that these seniors continue living comfortably at their homes by the care givers ensuring the home is clean as well as providing timely meals. Services offered therefore include.

•Preparing meals – This ensures your loved one will never go hungry at any time and help them with healthy meals.

•Light House-keeping – This simply refers to ensuring that the environment is clean and everything well organized.

•Laundry – This is a part of hygiene. The care giver therefore does the laundry for the client or helps them do it.

•Ironing – Although it may seem so simple and of less important as compared to other tasks, feeling clean and neat is vital for everyone; the elderly is no exception.

•Waste disposal – This involves emptying trash bins as well as ensuring there is no odor.
